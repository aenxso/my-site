About me

I graduated with a Software Systems degree, finished a co-op, aced the hard courses... and still wondered if I deserved the paper they handed me.

Post-grad, the job market froze. So did my confidence. But instead of chasing another checkbox, I went deep:

Into code.

Into psychology.

Into the quiet panic that comes with realizing you don’t want to fake competence. You want to earn it.

Since then, I’ve built systems, studied conversion, explored security, launched digital content, and learned how to build things that feel as good as they function.

Not everything I’ve built has made money. But everything I’ve built has made me better.

And that’s the version of me writing this. Ready for what’s next, grounded by what’s real.
